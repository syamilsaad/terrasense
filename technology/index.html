Our Technology

Advanced passive optical fiber sensing for continuous monitoring.

Optical Fiber Sensing

We leverage optical fiber sensing, fully passive with no electrical components at the sensor level. This reduces deployment cost including long-term maintenance and operational costs.

Comprehensive Structural Monitoring

We design customized instrumentation schemes that integrate both surface and subsurface monitoring, including tilt, displacement, and deformation measurements.

Modular & Scalable Monitoring Architecture

Our monitoring systems are built on a modular architecture, allowing sensors, data acquisition units, and power systems to be upgraded as monitoring needs evolve.
