Pilot Deployments & Projects

Field-proven optical fiber sensing deployments across slopes and infrastructure.

Blue Valley, Cameron Highlands

Deployment of optical fiber–based tilt and deformation monitoring system on a high-risk slope. The system provided continuous real-time data to detect early movement and assess slope stability under varying rainfall conditions.

Client: Private Developer

Monitoring Type: Slope deformation & early warning

University Malaya Medical Centre (PPUM)

Structural and slope monitoring project integrating optical fiber sensors for subsurface displacement tracking. The project demonstrated the feasibility of replacing manual inspection with automated sensing.

Client: Public Institution

Monitoring Type: Structural & slope monitoring

Bukit Nanas, Kuala Lumpur

Pilot study on urban slope monitoring using distributed optical fiber sensing to capture ground movement beneath vegetated slopes in a dense urban environment.

Partner: Universiti Malaya

Monitoring Type: Distributed deformation sensing
