Our Pilot Projects

Proven real-world deployments across Malaysia's critical infrastructure.

Kuala Lumpur

Academy of Malay Studies

Monitoring of a previously collapsed slope with signs of further movement seen in nearby structures. A total of 10 optical fiber-based tiltmeters were deployed and monitoring is still ongoing.

Kuala Lumpur

Outpatient New Wing, University Hospital (PPUM)

Slope and infrastructure monitoring adjacent to an active construction site. Two boreholes were monitored using our inclinometer probe and verified with conventional methods. Additionally, 2 tiltmeters monitor nearby building columns for inclination or vibration.

Cameron Highlands

Blue Valley, Cameron Highlands

A significant joint research collaboration with Pintas Utama Sdn Bhd to test real-time slope monitoring using optical fiber sensors. With 6 tiltmeters and 3 extensometers, this marked our expansion into the geotechnical sector.

Intelligent sensing solutions for a safer built environment.
