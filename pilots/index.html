Pilot Deployments

Demonstrated performance through real-world slope and infrastructure monitoring projects.

Selected Pilot Projects

TerraSense has conducted pilot deployments and monitoring campaigns across slopes and structural assets, integrating optical fiber sensing systems with geotechnical workflows to deliver actionable, real-time insights.

Hillslope Monitoring – Cameron Highlands

Deployment of FBG-based tiltmeters and subsurface inclinometer sensing to capture early-stage ground movement and validate system sensitivity under tropical environmental conditions.

Infrastructure Monitoring – Urban Asset

Real-time monitoring of structural tilt and deformation using passive optical fiber sensors, supporting condition assessment and risk-informed maintenance decisions.

What These Pilots Demonstrate

These pilot projects validate TerraSense’s ability to deliver continuous, high-resolution monitoring with minimal maintenance, enabling early warning, improved situational awareness, and seamless integration with existing engineering practices.
