Who We Are

TerraSense is a deep-tech startup specializing in real-time slope and infrastructure monitoring. We integrate optical fiber sensing with engineering workflows to provide early warnings, reduce risk, and support data-driven decision making.

Our Vision

To become a trusted technology partner in safeguarding infrastructure and communities through intelligent sensing.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, scalable, and industry-ready monitoring systems that integrate seamlessly with existing geotechnical practices.

Our Technology

TerraSense leverages distributed optical fiber sensing to enable continuous, spatially resolved monitoring of ground and structural behavior.

Optical Fiber Sensing

Immune to electromagnetic interference and capable of long-term deployment in harsh environments.

Subsurface Insight

Captures deformation and movement beneath the surface, where slope failures initiate.

Real-Time & Distributed

Continuous monitoring along the entire fiber length enables early detection and precise localization.
