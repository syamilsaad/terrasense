About TerraSense

Innovative monitoring solutions using optical fiber technology

Founded in 2025, TerraSense started as a research spin-off to commercialize optical fiber sensing for geotechnical applications. We are specialized in custom optical fiber sensor solution, where our team of engineers and researchers focuses on innovation and reliability.

Our Story

Research & Innovation

It began in the lab, where we spent years perfecting optical fiber sensor designs to withstand the toughest geotechnical conditions.

Homegrown Technology

We successfully developed our own range of tiltmeters and inclinometers, built specifically for Malaysia's unique terrain.

Field Validation

Our technology moved from the lab to the field, proving its reliability in monitoring high-risk slope areas across the country.

Industrial Application

Today, we simplify complex fiber-optic data into actionable insights, helping industries solve real-world monitoring problems.

Intelligent sensing solutions for a safer built environment. Specializing in high-precision optical fiber monitoring for slopes and structural assets.
