About TerraSense

TerraSense is a deep-tech startup providing real-time slope and infrastructure monitoring. Our team of engineers and researchers integrate optical fiber sensing with practical engineering workflows.

Core Values

Innovation, Reliability, and Safety. We are committed to providing actionable insights that reduce risk and improve decision-making.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, TerraSense started as a research spin-off to commercialize optical fiber sensing for geotechnical applications.
