Our Services

Professional monitoring solutions for geotechnical and structural needs.

Slope & Geotechnical Monitoring

Site-specific design and deployment of surface and subsurface instrumentations and monitoring systems, providing ground movement assessment using optical fiber sensing technology.

Structural Health Monitoring

Instrumentation installation and monitoring of infrastructures, bridges, and buildings to track tilt, deformation, and long-term structural behavior using optical fiber sensing technology.

Custom Optical Fiber Sensor

Design, fabrication, and customization of application-specific optical fiber sensors (tilt, strain, displacement, etc.) tailored to client conditions and performance requirements.

Sensor Installation & Data Acquisition

Field installation of optical fiber sensors and supporting hardware, including data loggers, industrial gateways, and power systems for reliable and continuous data acquisition.

Data Visualization, Analytics & Reporting

Development of monitoring dashboards, automated data processing, and periodic engineering reports to support the interpretation of complex monitoring behavior.

Consulting and R&D Support

Consultancy services on optical fiber system design, commissioning, and performance evaluation, including post-installation services and specialized R&D projects.
